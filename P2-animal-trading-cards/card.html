Horsehoe bat

In winter, it hibernates in underground sites. This species is considered to be sedentary.

  • Scientific Name: Rhinolophus blasii
  • Average Length: 46.5-56 mm
  • Average Lifespan: 9 years
  • Habitat: Warm limestone-based area

Threats to the species include loss of Mediterranean woodlands, disturbance and loss of underground habitats, and destruction of roost sites. In a number of range states the species is disturbed by tourist visits to caves and by use of the caves as shelters for livestock.
